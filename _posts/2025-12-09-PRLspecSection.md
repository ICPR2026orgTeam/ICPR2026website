<p>We are pleased to announce that, as for previous editions, a Special Section of <em><a href="https://www.sciencedirect.com/journal/pattern-recognition-letters">Elsevier Pattern Recognition Letters</a> (PRL)</em> will be dedicated to ICPR 2026.</p>

<p>This Special Section will host invited extended versions of the Best Paper and Best Student Paper from each ICPR 2026 track. Invited submissions will be required to include at least 30% new scientific contributions and will undergo a full regular peer-review process.</p>

<p>We warmly thank <strong>PRL</strong> and <strong>Elsevier</strong> for their continued support.</p>
