Workshops

Please note that all ICPR workshops will take place in the Louis Néel Building , rather than in the main conference convention center.

Selected Workshops

We thank the IAPR community for all submitted workshop proposals. The list of selected workshops with links to their respective webpages with submission instructions and more information is available below. For general queries on the workshops, please, contact workshop_chairs@icpr2026.org. For queries on a specific workshop, please, contact the respective organisers listed on their webpage.

Information about workshop registration and registration requirements for authors of accepted workshop papers can be found on the ICPR registration page.
