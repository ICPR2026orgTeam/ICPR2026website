Reserve your LunchBox

Still possible to reserve your Lunchbox

To help you enjoy a relaxed and convenient lunch during the conference, we highly recommend ordering one of our conference lunch boxes. With a 90 minute lunch break, staying in the Cité Internationale area is the easiest way to make the most of your time and avoid rushing back for the afternoon sessions. Nearby restaurants are also limited and typically cost €18 - 23 per meal (excluding drinks).

Even better, you can take your lunch box to the beautiful Parc de la Tête d'Or, Lyon's largest and most iconic park, located just a few minutes' walk from the Convention Centre. It's the perfect setting to relax, meet fellow participants, and enjoy one of the city's most treasured green spaces.

To add lunch boxes to your registration, simply follow this link: Link Lunch registration. Please place your lunch box order by 23 July to help us finalize catering arrangements.
