⚠️ Important Notice for Authors

Authors are required to follow all submission requirements as listed on the ICPR 2026 website paying particular attention to the "Instructions for Authors" webpage. Failure to comply with these requirements may result in rejection without review (i.e., a desk rejection). Questions regarding the submission requirements should be directed to the Program Chairs.

Important Dates

Main conference

  • Paper submission : January 10, 2026 AoE (UTC-12) – extended
  • Reviews sent to authors : March 2 2026
  • Author response (optional) : March 16, 2026
  • Author notification : March 31, 2026
  • Camera-ready submission : May 4, 2026

Competition proposal

  • Competition proposal : November 1st, 2025
  • Competition acceptance : December 1st, 2025

Doctorial Consortium

  • Submission deadline : May 2nd, 2026
  • Doctorial acceptance : May 15, 2026

Tutorial proposal

  • Tutorial proposal : February 28, 2026
  • Tutorial acceptance : March 31, 2026

Workshop proposal

  • Workshop proposal : November 30, 2025
  • Workshop acceptance : December 31, 2025
